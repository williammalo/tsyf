Transport Sylvester & Forget Inc. is a company established in Quebec, Canada since 1991; we provide specialized cross-border transportation and hold licenses to operate in Canada and the United States, both for general transportation, hazardous and waste materials. We have decided to expand and diversify our business and operations. We are concentrating in domestic transport and warehousing to complement the services we already offer to our existing and new customers. Transport Sylvester & Forget Inc. offers a high service reliability and confidence for everything concerning Full truck Load in Quebec, Ontario and the United States. This is why we’ve developed over the years, lasting business relationships. We presently have customers that have been with us for over fifteen years and still going strong. With a fleet of 80 late model trucks and over 200, 53’ air ride dry vans, a state-of-the art system of direct communications and satellite tracking allows us to give a reliable and efficient service to all our customers.
